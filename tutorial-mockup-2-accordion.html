Game Basics

Liap is a strategic card game where 4 players compete to capture pieces and score points by accurately predicting their wins.

🎯
Goal

Be the first to reach 50 points or have the most points after 20 rounds!

Game Flow

1. Deal Phase
Each player receives 8 random pieces
2. Declaration Phase
Predict how many piles you'll win (0-3)
3. Play Phase
Take turns playing 1-6 pieces. Highest total wins the pile!

Tie Breaker

If players play the same total value, the first player wins!

Scoring Rules

Hit Target
Base points = piles won
Bonus = piles won × 1 point
Total = Base + Bonus
Miss Target
Penalty = difference × 2
Example: Declared 3, won 1 = -4 points
💎
Special: The 0/0 Strategy

Declaring 0 and winning 0 piles gives you 6 points! It's risky but rewarding.

💡
Pro Tips & Strategies
🎯
Start Conservative

When learning, declare 1 pile. It's easier to hit and helps you understand the flow.

👀
Watch the Count

Track how many pieces others have played. If someone plays 6 pieces early, they might be going for 0!

🃏
Weak Hand Rule

No piece above 9 points? You can request a redeal! But beware - this doubles the round multiplier.

🧮
The Math

Total declarations can't equal 8. If 3 players declared 2, 2, and 3, the last player can't declare 1!

⭐
Red General Bonus

The player with the Red General starts first - use this advantage wisely!
