🏆 Scoring System

✅ Perfect Match
Declare 3, Win 3 = 3 + 5 = +8 points
❌ Miss Target
Declare 3, Win 1 = -(3-1) = -2 points
💎 Zero Strategy
Declare 0, Win 0 = +3 points
🎯 Declaration Rule
Total declarations ≠ 8 (forces competition)

💡 Pro Strategies

🎯 Strategic Forfeit
Play invalid combos to lose tricks on purpose
📊 Count Cards
Track which high pieces have been played
🔮 Read Opponents
Watch declaration patterns to predict plays
💎 Bad Hand?
Go for 0/0 when you have weak pieces

⚠️ Common Mistakes

❌ Trying to win every trick
❌ Forgetting the declaration total ≠ 8
❌ Not using strategic forfeits
❌ Playing high pieces too early
