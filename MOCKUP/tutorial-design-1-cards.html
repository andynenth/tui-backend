Welcome to Castellan!

Master the art of prediction in this strategic card game

🎯 Objective
Predict how many tricks you'll win each round and match your prediction exactly for bonus points!
👥 Players
4 players compete with 8 pieces each per round
🏆 Victory
First player to reach 50 points wins the game
💡 Key Strategy
It's not about winning the most tricks — it's about winning exactly what you predict!

📏 Rule
Higher value pieces beat lower ones. When values are equal, the first played wins!

Phase 1: Preparation

1 Deal Phase
Each player receives 8 random pieces from the deck
2 Check for Weak Hand
If your strongest piece is 象 (9) or lower, you may request a redeal for all players
⚠️ Warning: Requesting redeal reduces your score multiplier!
3 Starting Player
The player holding 帥 (General) will lead the first trick

Phase 2: Declaration

1 Make Your Prediction
Each player declares how many tricks (0-8) they expect to win this round
! The Forcing Rule
Total declarations cannot equal 8! This guarantees competition
Example Scenario
Players A, B, C declared: 2, 2, 3 (total = 7)
Player D CANNOT declare 1 (would make total 8)

Phase 3: Playing

1 Leader Plays First
Play 1-6 pieces in a valid combination
2 Others Follow
Must play same number of pieces (can be invalid to forfeit)
3 Winner Takes All
Highest valid play wins the trick and leads next
💡 Strategic Forfeit
Play invalid combinations intentionally to avoid winning unwanted tricks!

Scoring System

✓ Perfect Match
Win exactly what you declared = Tricks + 5 bonus
Example: Declare 2, Win 2 = 2 + 5 = +7 points
✗ Miss Target
Penalty = -|difference| between declared and won
Example: Declare 3, Win 1 = -(3-1) = -2 points
💎 Zero Strategy
Declaring 0 and winning 0 tricks = +3 points
High risk strategy for weak hands!
