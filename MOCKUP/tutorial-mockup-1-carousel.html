Welcome to Liap!

Liap is a strategic card game where you compete to capture pieces and score points by accurately predicting your captures.

Higher value pieces beat lower ones. Same value? First played wins!

How to Play

Each round has 3 phases:

1. Deal: Each player gets 8 pieces

2. Declare: Predict how many piles you'll win (0-3)

3. Play: Take turns playing 1-6 pieces. Highest total wins!

The key: Your declaration total must not equal 8!
