How to Play Liap

Learn the basics in 5 minutes

Welcome to Liap!

Liap is a strategic trick-taking game where prediction meets skill

4 players compete over 20 rounds to reach 50 points

Objective: Score the most points by accurately predicting how many tricks (piles) you'll win

Know Your Army

General 11 points
Advisor 10 points
Elephant 9 points
Horse 8 points
Chariot 7 points
Cannon 6 points
Soldiers 1-5 points

Higher value pieces beat lower ones.
Same value? First played wins!

Phase 1: Preparation

🃏

Deal 8 Pieces

Each player receives 8 random pieces

🔍

Check Your Hand

Weak hand = no piece > 9 points

🔄

Redeal Option

Request new deal (costs multiplier)

Note: The player with the Red General starts the round!

Phase 2: Declaration

🎯

Declare 0-8 Piles

Predict how many you'll win

⚠️

Important Rule

Total declarations ≠ 8 (forces competition)

Example:
If 3 players declared 2, 2, 3 (total=7)
The last player CANNOT declare 1

Phase 3: Playing

⚔️

Take Turns Playing

Starter plays 1-6 pieces (must be valid)

🎴

Others Must Match

Play same number (can forfeit with invalid)

🏆

Winner Takes All

Highest wins pile, starts next turn

Forfeit Strategy: Non-starters can play invalid combinations to dump unwanted pieces!

Valid Play Types

Single
Pair
Three-of-a-kind
Straight

Remember:
• Pair = same name & color
• Three-of-a-kind = 3 soldiers only
• Straight = consecutive ranks
• 4-6 pieces have extended variations

Only the starter must play valid combinations!
